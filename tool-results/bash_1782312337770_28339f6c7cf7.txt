comprehensive data on farmers, including biographical information, location details, 
and farm-speciﬁc data. This digital tool enables efﬁcient capture and organization of 
farmer proﬁles, supporting agricultural programs, outreach, and data-driven 
decision-making. 
Core Features: 
●Farmer Biography Collection:  Captures detailed biographical information 
about farmers. 
●Location Data Collection:  Records geographical location details of farms. 
●Farm Data Management:  Collects and manages data on farm characteristics, 
practices, and assets. 
●Data Storage and Retrieval: Securely stores farmer proﬁles for easy access and 
analysis. 
●Ofﬂine Data Capture:  Enables data collection in areas with limited 
connectivity, with syncing capabilities.

FAQs 
1.Q: Does the App work ofﬂine? 
A: Yes, the app functions ofﬂine. Data connection is required only for initial login and uploading proﬁled data. 
2.Q: How do I obtain login credentials? 
Credentials are sent via SMS after you've been proﬁled into the system. If you forget or lose your password, contact an 
Administrator for password reset assistance. 
3.Q: Some farmers I'm proﬁling prefer not to share certain data or photos. How do I handle this? 
A: For text ﬁelds, enter "N/A" to indicate non-applicability. For numeric ﬁelds, use "0". If a photo is required but the farmer 
is unwilling, you can capture an image of a plain background or a blank paper to proceed. 
4.Q: I'm encountering an error when trying to log in (Inconsistent User Data Found…). What does it mean? 
A: The error typically indicates you're not associated with a group. Contact an Administrator to ensure you're attached to 
a relevant group, then retry login. 
5. Q: Can I edit a farmer's proﬁle after it's been saved? 
A: Yes, proﬁles can typically be edited. click on the proﬁle from the list to edit the data

Purchases Module 
Overview:  The Merchant Purchase Module enables users to perform purchases via the 
mobile application, facilitating efﬁcient transactions for farmers who sell their products. 
This module requires a three-user workﬂow to ensure accuracy and security. 
User Roles and Responsibilities 
1. Initiator (Agent). The Initiator is responsible for initiating transactions by recording 
farmer purchases in the system. 
2. Reviewer. The Reviewer veriﬁes and reviews the initiated transactions for accuracy and 
completeness, then forwards them for approval. 
3. Approver.  The Approver reviews and approves the transactions. Once approved, the 
system disburses mobile money payments to the farmers' phones.
